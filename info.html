Pierce Dining Hall

Breakfast:

From 7am to 11am, breakfast is served in Pierce. From the omlet station, to yogurt, to cereal, and even regular eggs, bacon, and hashbrowns, Pierce has endless choices to choose from!

Lunch:

From 11am to 4pm, lunch is served with a ton of options! There is freshly-baked pizza, pasta, sandwiches, salads, hot, hamburgers and even french fries on the daily! Besides from the grill for a more personal touch, there are different entrees every day for lunch to keep up the variety.

Dinner:

From 4pm to 12pm, dinner is the exact same as lunch. The only major difference between the two is what's served as entrees for a variety!

Meal Exchange

As apart of the Stevens dining program, a membership holder is allowed to exchange access to Pierce for a meal at any Stevens retail location during meal period. If a meal exchange is used during a meal period, you cannot swipe at another location (including Perice). Meal exchanges inclue a drink, entree and a side.

Meal Exchange Periods:

  • Breakfast: 7:00 am – 11:00 am
  • Lunch: 11:00 am – 4:00 pm
  • Dinner: 4:00 pm – 9:30 pm

Meal Exchange Locations:

  • America's Cup
  • Colonel John’s
  • Red and Gray Cafe

Washington Street Wednesday

Washington Street Wednesday (WSW) allows students with a meal plan, to exchange one meal per week at participating WSW vendors on Wednesday's. Each vendor has a prepared entree, side and beverage.

WSW Vendors:

  • Benny Tudino's
  • Makai Poke Co
  • Flatbread Grill
  • Stacks Pancake House
  • Hansel ‘N Griddle
